2. SKU Validator & Catalog

Goal: Validate SKUs against a catalog and prevent invalid cart entries.

Build:

Catalog.get(sku) -> Product(name, price: Money)

Cart.add(sku, qty) consults Catalog.

Edges: unknown SKU, out-of-stock flag, price changes mid-add (assume snapshot at add).

Tests: unknown SKU rejected; in-stock passes; toggling stock blocks add.
